Problem 1: Mean (Average)

Find the mean of the following data set:

12, 15, 18, 22, 13, 16, 19
Mean = (sum of all values) √∑ (number of values). Add all numbers and divide by 7.

Problem 2: Median

Find the median of the following data set:

8, 12, 5, 19, 7, 15, 10, 9, 14
First arrange in order: 5, 7, 8, 9, 10, 12, 14, 15, 19. The median is the middle value.

Problem 5: Combinations

In how many ways can you choose 3 students from a class of 8 students?

Use the combination formula: C(n,r) = n! √∑ (r!(n-r)!)

C(8,3) = 8! √∑ (3! √ó 5!) = (8 √ó 7 √ó 6) √∑ (3 √ó 2 √ó 1) = 336 √∑ 6 = 56
